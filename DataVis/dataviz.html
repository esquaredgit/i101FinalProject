This is our data vizualizations page. We have included data we collected through our survey as well as vizuals we found from other sources to help maximize the efficiency of our solution design.

This chart shows the how likely the people we surveyed are to use an app to help navigate a farmers market. The data shows that 17% of the 35 people said they were extremely likley to use the app. 24% said they were very likely to use the app. While less then 15% said they were either not very likely or not likely to use the app.

This pie chart represents the number of people who would use augmented reality in order to check the prices of products at a stand. This data is helpful because we know augmented reality is a feature that our users would utalize on a regualar basis. The data shows that almost 90% of the respondents would take advantage of the augmented reality feature.

This table is from Establishing and operating a community farmers market. The table shows why people shop at farmers market with 46% reporting the reason they shop at farmers markets is for fresher and better quality produce.
