Process of solution

In order to promote the goods and services offered at local farmers’ markets, and to facilitate interaction between the farmers and their customers, we are proposing a mutli-faceted solution centered around a mobile application. This app would offer a variety of features for both farmers and customers that would result in greater information flow and customer engagement. The following are some examples of the features offered by the app:

Farmers/Producers:

  • Farmers can push out notifications to customers who follow their booth on the app.
  • Farmers can keep track of inventory sold, and the app would use predictive analytics to let them know what customers like/don’t like.
  • The app can keep profile data for each user and push products/producers that users might like, but haven’t favorited yet.
  • The app uses machine learning/predictive techniques to let farmers know what types of products would be more successful at certain locations.

Customers:

  • Users can favorite certain booths/producers, products, etc. and get notified when these favorites are present in their area (using geofencing, for example).
  • Users can search by booth/producer, product.
  • Page that shows daily happenings related to farmers’ markets in the area.
  • We could also implement an incentive system that would allow users to check in at individual booths and receive loyalty points of some sort, redeemable for a discount or something else of the farmer’s choice.
